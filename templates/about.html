Who We Are

Our Vision

At CRIBDASH, we believe finding the perfect rental should be easy, fast, and hassle-free. As students, we understand the challenges that come with searching for rental spaces—long hours spent scrolling through endless listings and contacting landlords. Our mission is simple: To help students and tenants find their ideal homes with just a few clicks. We provide a platform that simplifies the rental process, making it quicker and more efficient for students to find their next home.

  • Easy and Fast Rental Search: CRIBDASH aims to streamline the rental process, saving Tenants time and effort.
  • Student-Centric Approach: Understanding the unique needs of students and Tenants, CRIBDASH offers a platform tailored to their specific requirements..
  • Simplified Rental Process: CRIBDASH simplifies the rental process by providing a user-friendly platform and efficient tools.
  • Ideal Home Finder: CRIBDASH helps Tenants find their ideal homes by connecting them with suitable rental properties.

Our Story

CRIBDASH started as a solution to a common problem faced by students—finding affordable and suitable accommodation. We know that as students, time and resources are limited, and finding a place to live shouldn't be a stressful task. Our founders, a group of tech-savvy students, decided to create a platform that would bridge the gap between students and landlords. By connecting students directly with landlords through an easy-to-use interface, CRIBDASH makes it possible to browse available listings, read detailed descriptions, view images, and even contact landlords—all within a few minutes.

Our goal is to make the entire rental search process faster and more convenient, so students can focus on what truly matters—their studies and life experiences. Whether you’re looking for a small apartment near campus or a shared house with friends, CRIBDASH is here to help you find the perfect place. We are committed to making the rental experience smooth, transparent, and as stress-free as possible.
